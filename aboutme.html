Biography

I am a third-year University of Toronto Mississauga student with years of experience in UX/XI design, front-end development as well as graphic design. Right now, I’m double majoring respectively in CCIT and TCS programs, both preparing students to be changemakers in business, technology, media, and communication.

CCIT and TCS are special programs cooperating with Sheridan College, which offers students practical training that enhances their academic skills and prepares them to go to the workplace.
